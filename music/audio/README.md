## Free Audio Sources

1. **Internet Archive** (Free & Public Domain)
   - https://archive.org/details/ChildrensMusic
   - Download songs and rename them

2. **Free Music Archive**
   - https://freemusicarchive.org

3. **YouTube to MP3**
   - Use yt-dlp: `yt-dlp "YouTube URL --extract-audio --audio-format mp3`

## Note

If local audio files are not available, the player will use online demo audio as fallback.
